About the project

PupPeroni is a brand that embraces a unique bond with your canine companion. The project involves delving into the brand essence and incorporating e-commerce solutions seamlessly into the current website. It also focuses on the integration of SaaS features and introducing an inventive concept to enhance customer engagement, encouraging them to return to the e-commerce store for purchases.

Phases

The project is structured into four distinct phases, each playing a crucial role in crafting the brand journey while preserving its inherent brand identity.

About PupPeroni

Established in 1897 in Orrville, OH, the Pup-Peroni® brand commemorates our unique bond with our four legged best friend. Known for crafting a range of flavorful dog treats, Pup-Peroni began as a division of Del Monte before being acquired by Big Heart Pet Brands. Subsequently, in 2015, the J.M. Smucker Company acquired Big Heart Pet Brands.

Innovation Pitch :
Pet Profile & Autoship


Acknowledging the recurring inconvenience of monthly pet food purchases, we introduce the Pet Profile feature. This functionality enables customers to craft individualized dog profiles by providing details such as their pet's name, age, breed, birthday, and preferred PupPeroni treats. Once the treats are chosen for each unique pet profile, they will be automatically dispatched to the customer every month. This concept not only enhances customer loyalty and trust but also contributes to a boost in product sales.
